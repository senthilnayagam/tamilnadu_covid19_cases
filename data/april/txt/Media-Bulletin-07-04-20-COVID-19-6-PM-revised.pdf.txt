                      Media Bulletin 07.04.2020 6 PM

          Daily report on Public Health measures taken for COVID-19

      In Tamil Nadu, till date 2,10,538 passengers were screened at the
Airports of Chennai, Trichy, Madurai, and Coimbatore. Total number of
persons put on home quarantine till yesterday was 91,851. Till date 27,416
passengers have completed 28 days follow-up. As on date 92,531
passengers are under home quarantine for 28 days. Currently, 253
asymptomatic     passengers   from    highly   affected   countries   are    being
quarantined in quarantine facilities near airport and 1864 are under
hospital isolation.
      Till now 5305 samples have been taken from the passengers and sent
for testing at King Institute of Preventive Medicine & Research, Guindy
Chennai, National Institute of Virology, Pune, Theni VRDL, Tiruvarur VRDL,
Tirunelveli VRDL, RGGGH VDRL, Stanley VRDL, Coimbatore VRDL, Salem
VRDL, Villupuram VRDL, IRT Perundurai medical college, Madurai VRDL,
Trichy VRDL, Neuberg Ehrlich, CMC Vellore, YRG Chennai, Micro labs,
Coimbatore, SRMC, Apollo Chennai and Hitech Diagnostic Centre Chennai.
690 persons are tested positive till date. 4414 samples are tested negative.
Testing of 201 samples are under process.
      The general public are advised to adhere to health advisories and
travel advisories issued by the state Government. Public should follow the
cough etiquette by covering the face using handkerchief / towel while
sneezing / coughing. Do frequent handwashing with soap and water.
