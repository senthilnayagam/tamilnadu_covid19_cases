                        Media Bulletin 14.05.2020

         Daily report on Public Health measures taken for COVID-19

      Till now 2,91,432 samples have been taken and sent for testing.
COVID-19 testing is done in 38 Government and 20 Private labs all over
Tamil Nadu. Two Private labs (Bioline Laboratory, Coimbatore & Premier
Health Centre, Chennai) has been approved for COVID-19 testing today.
9,674 persons have tested positive till date. 2,81,001 samples were tested
negative. Testing of 757 samples are under process. 11,409 samples are
repeat samples. 2,240 COVID-19 positive patients have been discharged
following treatment till today. Hence, 7,365 active cases are under treatment
as on today.


      The general public are advised to adhere to health advisories and
travel advisories issued by the state Government. Public should follow the
cough etiquette by covering the face using handkerchief / towel while
sneezing/ coughing. Do frequent hand washing with soap and water.
