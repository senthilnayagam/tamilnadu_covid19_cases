                       Media Bulletin 16.05.2020

         Daily report on Public Health measures taken for COVID-19

      Till now 3,13,639 samples have been taken and sent for testing.
COVID-19 testing is done in 39 Government and 22 Private labs all over
Tamil Nadu. One Government Lab (Government District Headquarters
Hospital, Tiruvallur) and Two Private Labs (Royal Care Super Speciality
Hospital, Coimbatore & Sundaram Medical Foundation, Chennai) have been
approved for COVID-19 testing today. 10,585 persons have tested positive
till date. 3,02,523 samples were tested negative. Testing of 531 samples are
under process. 14,463 samples are repeat samples. 3,538 COVID-19 positive
patients have been discharged following treatment till today. Hence, 6,970
active cases are under treatment as on today.


      The general public are advised to adhere to health advisories and
travel advisories issued by the state Government. Public should follow the
cough etiquette by covering the face using handkerchief / towel while
sneezing/ coughing. Do frequent hand washing with soap and water.
