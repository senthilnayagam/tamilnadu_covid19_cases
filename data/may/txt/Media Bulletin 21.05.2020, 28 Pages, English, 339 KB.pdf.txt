                        Media Bulletin 21.05.2020

         Daily report on Public Health measures taken for COVID-19

      Till now 3,72,532 samples have been taken and sent for testing.
COVID-19 testing is done in 41 Government and 25 Private labs all over
Tamil Nadu.    One Government lab (Central Leather Research Institute,
Adayar, Chennai) & Two Private labs (Apollo Speciality Hospitals, Madurai
and Orbito Asia Diagnostics, Coimbatore) have been approved for COVID-19
testing today. 13,967 persons have tested positive till date. 3,57,898
samples were tested negative. Testing of 667 samples are under process.
16,639 samples are repeat samples. 6,282 COVID-19 positive patients have
been discharged following treatment till today. Hence, 7,588 active cases are
under treatment as on today.


      The general public are advised to adhere to health advisories and
travel advisories issued by the state Government. Public should follow the
cough etiquette by covering the face using handkerchief / towel while
sneezing/ coughing. Do frequent hand washing with soap and water.
