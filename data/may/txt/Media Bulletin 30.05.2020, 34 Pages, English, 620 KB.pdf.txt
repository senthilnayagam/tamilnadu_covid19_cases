                        Media Bulletin 30.05.2020

         Daily report on Public Health measures taken for COVID-19

      Till now 4,79,155 samples have been taken and sent for testing.
COVID-19 testing is done in 43 Government and 29 Private labs all over
Tamil Nadu. One private lab (Karpaga Vinayaga Institute Of Medical
Sciences, Chengalpattu) has been approved for COVID-19 testing today.
21,184 persons have tested positive till date. 4,57,405 samples were tested
negative. Testing of 566 samples are under process. 21,922 samples are
repeat samples. 12,000 COVID-19 positive patients have been discharged
following treatment till today. Hence, 9,021 active cases are under treatment
as on today.


      The general public are advised to adhere to health advisories and
travel advisories issued by the state Government. Public should follow the
cough etiquette by covering the face using handkerchief / towel while
sneezing/ coughing. Do frequent hand washing with soap and water.
