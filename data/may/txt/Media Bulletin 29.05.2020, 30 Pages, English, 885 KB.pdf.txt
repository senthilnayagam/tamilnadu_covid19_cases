                        Media Bulletin 29.05.2020

         Daily report on Public Health measures taken for COVID-19

      Till now 4,66,550 samples have been taken and sent for testing.
COVID-19 testing is done in 43 Government and 28 Private labs all over
Tamil Nadu. One Government lab (C.D Hospital, Chennai) have been
approved for COVID-19 testing today. 20,246 persons have tested positive
till date. 4,45,668 samples were tested negative. Testing of 636 samples are
under process. 21,356 samples are repeat samples. 11,313 COVID-19
positive patients have been discharged following treatment till today. Hence,
8,776 active cases are under treatment as on today.


      The general public are advised to adhere to health advisories and
travel advisories issued by the state Government. Public should follow the
cough etiquette by covering the face using handkerchief / towel while
sneezing/ coughing. Do frequent hand washing with soap and water.
