                       Media Bulletin 13.05.2020

         Daily report on Public Health measures taken for COVID-19

      Till now 2,79,467 samples have been taken and sent for testing.
COVID-19 testing is done in 38 Government and 18 Private labs all over
Tamil Nadu. One Private lab (Nu-Med Labs, Kanchipuram) has been
approved for COVID-19 testing today. 9,227 persons have tested positive till
date. 2,69,758 samples were tested negative. Testing of 482 samples are
under process. 11,217 samples are repeat samples. 2,176 COVID-19 positive
patients have been discharged following treatment till today. Hence, 6,984
active cases are under treatment as on today.


      The general public are advised to adhere to health advisories and
travel advisories issued by the state Government. Public should follow the
cough etiquette by covering the face using handkerchief / towel while
sneezing/ coughing. Do frequent hand washing with soap and water.
