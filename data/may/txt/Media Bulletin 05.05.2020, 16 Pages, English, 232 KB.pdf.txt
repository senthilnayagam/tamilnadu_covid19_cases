                        Media Bulletin 05.05.2020

         Daily report on Public Health measures taken for COVID-19



      Till now 1,74,828 samples have been taken and sent for testing.
COVID-19 testing is done in 36 Government and 16 Private labs all over
Tamil Nadu. Two new private labs Doctors Diagnostics Centre Tiruchi and
Kovai Medical Center & Hospital Coimbatore are added. 4,058 persons have
tested positive till date. 1,70,174 samples were tested negative. Testing of
596 samples are under process. 9,637 samples are repeat samples. 1,485
COVID-19 positive patients have been discharged following treatment till
today. Hence, 2,537 active cases are under treatment as on today.


      The general public are advised to adhere to health advisories and
travel advisories issued by the state Government. Public should follow the
cough etiquette by covering the face using handkerchief / towel while
sneezing/ coughing. Do frequent handwashing with soap and water.
