                       Media Bulletin 18.05.2020

         Daily report on Public Health measures taken for COVID-19

     Till now 3,37,841 samples have been taken and sent for testing.
COVID-19 testing is done in 39 Government and 22 Private labs all over
Tamil Nadu. 11,760 persons have tested positive till date. 3,25,546 samples
were tested negative. Testing of 535 samples are under process. 15,333
samples are repeat samples. 4,406 COVID-19 positive patients have been
discharged following treatment till today. Hence, 7,270 active cases are
under treatment as on today.


     The general public are advised to adhere to health advisories and
travel advisories issued by the state Government. Public should follow the
cough etiquette by covering the face using handkerchief / towel while
sneezing/ coughing. Do frequent hand washing with soap and water.
