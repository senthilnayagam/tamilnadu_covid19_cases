                       Media Bulletin 22.05.2020

         Daily report on Public Health measures taken for COVID-19

      Till now 3,85,185 samples have been taken and sent for testing.
COVID-19 testing is done in 41 Government and 26 Private labs all over
Tamil Nadu.   One Private lab (Lifecell International Pvt Ltd, Chennai) has
been approved for COVID-19 testing today. 14,753 persons have tested
positive till date. 3,69,929 samples were tested negative. Testing of 503
samples are under process. 17,246 samples are repeat samples. 7,128
COVID-19 positive patients have been discharged following treatment till
today. Hence, 7,524 active cases are under treatment as on today.


      The general public are advised to adhere to health advisories and
travel advisories issued by the state Government. Public should follow the
cough etiquette by covering the face using handkerchief / towel while
sneezing/ coughing. Do frequent hand washing with soap and water.
