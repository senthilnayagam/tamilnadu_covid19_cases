                       Media Bulletin 12.05.2020

         Daily report on Public Health measures taken for COVID-19

      Till now 2,66,687 samples have been taken and sent for testing.
COVID-19 testing is done in 38 Government and 17 Private labs all over
Tamil Nadu. One Government lab (Government Kallakurichi Hospital) and
One Private lab (Chettinad Hospital and Research Institute, Chengalpattu)
has been approved for COVID-19 testing today. 8,718 persons have tested
positive till date. 2,56,720 samples were tested negative. Testing of 1,249
samples are under process. 11,103 samples are repeat samples. 2,134
COVID-19 positive patients have been discharged following treatment till
today. Hence, 6,520 active cases are under treatment as on today.


      The general public are advised to adhere to health advisories and
travel advisories issued by the state Government. Public should follow the
cough etiquette by covering the face using handkerchief / towel while
sneezing/ coughing. Do frequent hand washing with soap and water.
