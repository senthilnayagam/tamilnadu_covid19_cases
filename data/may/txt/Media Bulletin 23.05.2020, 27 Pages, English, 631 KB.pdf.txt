                       Media Bulletin 23.05.2020

         Daily report on Public Health measures taken for COVID-19

      Till now 3,97,340 samples have been taken and sent for testing.
COVID-19 testing is done in 41 Government and 27 Private labs all over
Tamil Nadu. One Private lab (Vivek Laboratories, Kanyakumari) has been
approved for COVID-19 testing today. 15,512 persons have tested positive
till date. 3,81,216 samples were tested negative. Testing of 612 samples are
under process. 17,529 samples are repeat samples. 7,491 COVID-19 positive
patients have been discharged following treatment till today. Hence, 7,915
active cases are under treatment as on today.


      The general public are advised to adhere to health advisories and
travel advisories issued by the state Government. Public should follow the
cough etiquette by covering the face using handkerchief / towel while
sneezing/ coughing. Do frequent hand washing with soap and water.
