                         Media Bulletin 20.06.2020

          Daily report on Public Health measures taken for COVID-19

COVID-19 testing is done in 45 Government and 40 Private labs all over Tamil
Nadu. Two private labs (Meenakshi Medical College Hospital and Research
Institute, Kanchipuram & Laboratory Services, Sri Ramakrishna Hospital,
Sidhapudur, Coimbatore) have been approved for COVID-19 testing today.
56,845 persons have tested positive till date. 8,03,824 samples were tested
negative. Testing of 542 samples are under process.

       The general public are advised to adhere to health advisories and
travel advisories issued by the state Government. Public should follow the
cough etiquette by covering the face using handkerchief / towel while
sneezing/ coughing. Do frequent hand washing with soap and water.
