                        Media Bulletin 07.06.2020

         Daily report on Public Health measures taken for COVID-19

      COVID-19 testing is done in 44 Government and 32 Private labs all
over Tamil Nadu. Two private labs (Central Laboratory, Sree Balaji Medical
College and Hospital, Chennai & Aarthi Scans and Labs, Vadapalani,
Chennai) have been approved for COVID-19 testing today. 31,667 persons
have tested positive till date. 5,60,890 samples were tested negative. Testing
of 413 samples are under process.
       The general public are advised to adhere to health advisories and
travel advisories issued by the state Government. Public should follow the
cough etiquette by covering the face using handkerchief / towel while
sneezing/ coughing. Do frequent hand washing with soap and water.
