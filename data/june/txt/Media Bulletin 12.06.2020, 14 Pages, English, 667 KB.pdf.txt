                        Media Bulletin 12.06.2020

         Daily report on Public Health measures taken for COVID-19

      COVID-19 testing is done in 45 Government and 33 Private labs all
over Tamil Nadu. One Government lab (Government Hospital of Thoracic
Medicine, Chennai) has been approved for COVID-19 testing today. 40,698
persons have tested positive till date. 6,32,656 samples were tested
negative. Testing of 552 samples are under process.
       The general public are advised to adhere to health advisories and
travel advisories issued by the state Government. Public should follow the
cough etiquette by covering the face using handkerchief / towel while
sneezing/ coughing. Do frequent hand washing with soap and water.
