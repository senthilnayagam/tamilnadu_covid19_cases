                        Media Bulletin 13.06.2020

         Daily report on Public Health measures taken for COVID-19

       COVID-19 testing is done in 45 Government and 34 Private labs all
over Tamil Nadu. One Private lab (Anderson Diagnostics and Labs, Chennai)
has been approved for COVID-19 testing today. 42,687 persons have tested
positive till date. 6,48,545 samples were tested negative. Testing of 585
samples are under process.
       The general public are advised to adhere to health advisories and
travel advisories issued by the state Government. Public should follow the
cough etiquette by covering the face using handkerchief / towel while
sneezing/ coughing. Do frequent hand washing with soap and water.
