                        Media Bulletin 24.06.2020

        Daily report on Public Health measures taken for COVID-19

       Sample testing for COVID-19 have crossed 32,000 and it stands at
32,079 for the last 24 hours period in the state. For Greater Chennai
Corporation alone, 9,371 samples were tested. COVID-19 testing is done
in 47 Government and 41 Private labs all over Tamil Nadu.               One
Government lab (Government Head Quarters Hospital, Krishnagiri) has been
approved for COVID-19 testing today. 67,468 persons have tested positive
till date. 9,08,292 samples were tested negative. Testing of 671 samples are
under process.

      The general public are advised to adhere to health advisories
and travel advisories issued by the state Government. Public should follow
the cough etiquette by covering the face using handkerchief / towel while
sneezing/ coughing. Do frequent hand washing with soap and water.
